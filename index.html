About US!

Project ALAS is the social service arm of UP ACES that utilizes in-depth and concrete application of engineering methods to solve real-life problems. In 2016, the project was officially launched by the said organization with a vision of creating meaningful and long-term impacts.

Through the years, Project ALAS was able to successfully carry out projects on water filtration and waste management control inside and outside the UP Community. In 2021, Project ALAS took a big leap and has started an initiative to raise awareness and empower resilient and sustainable communities. Still carrying on its initiative, this year, Project ALAS made an effort to concretize all of the plans made in the previous year.

In partnership with 4P For People Foundation and Base Builds, the goal of giving Hope for a Better Home to families from Tondo, Manila was actualized through the construction of a Community Building in Nasugbu, Batangas with the use of the sustainable Cement-Bamboo Frame Technology (CBFT).

Alleviating Lives. Advancing Solutions. This is what Project ALAS stands for. And with your support, we will stand stronger and help more communities stand on their own feet, together.

Community Building

When we think of the cost of houses, we only think of money. But what about nature?
They pay for it too. 😔 In an effort to create housing that is economically viable, socially acceptable, technologically feasible, and environmentally compatible (Ibem & Aduwo, 2015), sustainable houses were built. Standing with this movement is Project ALAS. Our joint project with 4P For People Foundation, the Community Building, will be built using sustainable materials to ease the burden of construction from our environment.🎋

Hope for a Better Home

Every building needs a strong foundation. That is why it is our greatest pleasure to have 4P For People Foundation build with us a better home in Nasugbu, Batangas!
Through this partnership, 4P For People Foundation’ assistance and rich experience in housing programs will provide an avenue for the realization of this year’s Project ALAS: Hope for a Better Home. Learn more about 4P For People Foundation’s initiatives here: https://4p.no/

Sabay sa Kaway ng Kawayan

Last November 19, Project ALAS held "Sabay sa Kaway ng Kawayan", a Face-to-Face Program for the scholars of 4P for People Foundation at Tondo, Manila.
The event aimed to impart values on 𝗲𝗻𝗴𝗶𝗻𝗲𝗲𝗿𝗶𝗻𝗴, 𝘀𝘂𝘀𝘁𝗮𝗶𝗻𝗮𝗯𝗶𝗹𝗶𝘁𝘆, and 𝗲𝗻𝘃𝗶𝗿𝗼𝗻𝗺𝗲𝗻𝘁𝗮𝗹 𝗮𝘄𝗮𝗿𝗲𝗻𝗲𝘀𝘀 to the students through activities such as house model making, film showing, and games!
We sincerely thank our partners, the Project ALAS A-team, the UP ACES members, and everyone who supports our cause, for making this event possible. Altogether, let us keep giving Hope for a Better Home.🏡
